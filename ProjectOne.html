WALK WITH ME

Imagine being half way across the world from your love ones and family members. Your travels are taking you across the highest mountains you have ever touched and the bluest lagoons your eyes have ever seen. Your feet has touched the soil of the Amazon and the paths of Machu Picchu. You stand in awe of the beautiful canvas known as the world. While on this journey your family yearns to immerse themselves and live vicariously through you. During this exact moment, Walk With Me is born.

Walk With Me is a platform that allows for family, friends + followers to travel directly alongside myself. Combined on one platform with access to all Facebook post, Twitter updates, photos and blog posts detailing my journey. With over 25 countries visited
